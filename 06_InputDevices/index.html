We also studied capacitance and made sensors with capacitors. For my sensor built using capacitors, I decided to build a flex sensor to measur the amount of force an object would have on the sensor based on its weight. How the sensor works is two pieces of copper stuck to pieces of velostat. As the pieces were compressed,they got closer together giving higher readings on the serial monitor. A quick few lines of code to get the readikng from the pieces and give an ouput were necessary but it was generally an easy make. ![](./parts.png) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; ![](./connection.png) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; ![](./sensor.png) I then used a scale in the lab to weigh various objects. I took down their weight in grams, converted their weight into newtons to measure force then recorded the output.
