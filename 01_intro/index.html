### Class 1: Introduction and Project Proposal ![](./sketches.png) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; ![](./door3.png) My Proposal: Automated Door Locker. Around the world, hundreds of thousands of break ins happen wheather it's into a car, house, shed, etc. People's property gets stolen and thousands of dollars of valuables disappear with no hope of them returning. What's a main reason for this? Locks. Wheather it's people forgetting to lock their dooe, a malfunctioning lock, or people just being lazy in general, the main cause of these break ins are failure to lock the area. My proposal for the automated door locker is one that sloves those things. You will never forget to lock your door as you wont ever have to remember to lock your door. The automated door locker uses a small plastic key that will send out a signal when the holder reaches a certain distance from the door. The key will then tell the door to lock. Nothing needs to be pressed on the key. The user just needs to attach it to their keychain and can forget about their worries of locking their doors. As for the locking itself, I was thinking of using either a conventional locking system or two sliders that block both sides of the door. Either way, the entire system will contain a square frame with a rectangular door. The locking system will most likley be embeded inside the frame and will push a blocker inside of the door. This project would be a start to some mechanical engineering principles and would also be a solution to less break ins inside of homes an cars. [Back](../index.html)
