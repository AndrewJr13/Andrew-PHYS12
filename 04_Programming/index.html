<br> <br> <head>Class 4: Microcontroller Programming</head> For Class 4, I decided to expand on my project from week 2. My week 3 project wasn't looking like it would be able to do much with this programming but I was inspired to do something with my bridge from week 2. So I decided to create a drawbridge. I had lots of ideas at first. My very first idea was to recreate my first bridge and use a flat piece of cardboard that would be pulled up by a motor to push both sides of the bridge outward. But before this I also had another roadblock. Without 3D printing I had no idea how I was going to make hinges for the bridge parts to fold upwards. I soon discovered living hinges though which are cardboard pieces with very small and precise cuts in them so they act like a real hinge. I watched a quick tutorial on youtube on how to make one and soon had two cut out for the bridge. ![](./hinges.png) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; ![](./hingebridge.png) [Back](../index.html).
