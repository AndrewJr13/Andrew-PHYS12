<br> <br> <head>Class 5: 3D Printing</head> I was very excited for this weeks class. I have always been intrested in 3D printing but often didn't know how to start. For this week's class I started by doing a very short 10 minute print. I designed a hollowed out cube in Fusion 360 and uploaded it to the Prusa slicer. At first 3D design on Fusion seemed tough. I was very used to doing sketches in a single plane and then extruding them to a specific width. This time I had to do sketches in different planes, join pieces, and make cuts as well.
